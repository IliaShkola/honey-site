Description

Easiest, fastest and stylish way to check and export data from IFC files.
A Python-based TUI desktop app that helps you browse and export data from IFC files quickly and beautifully.
Built with IfcOpenShell and Textual.

Features

Browse & Explore

Drill down into your IFC model with an intuitive TUI explorer that lets you jump between entities instantly.

Export Anywhere

Export data to JSON, CSV or XML with a single command to slot straight into your workflow.

Cross-platform Ready

Runs natively on Windows, macOS and Linux – just download and go.

Blazing Fast & Lightweight

Powered by pure-Python and IfcOpenShell, Honey opens even large models in seconds while staying under 10 MB.
